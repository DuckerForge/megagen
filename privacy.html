Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Megagen mobile app (the "App") handles. Megagen is a non-custodial Solana app. We do not require an account, email, phone number, or any identity verification (KYC), and we do not knowingly collect personal information.

No account, no personal data

There is no sign-up. We do not collect your name, email, phone, contacts, location, or device advertising identifiers. The App contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Your wallet address

To build and route transactions and to show balances, the App uses your Solana wallet's public address. A public wallet address is already visible to anyone on the public Solana blockchain. We never access your private keys or seed phrase — those stay in your wallet.

Public activity feed & leaderboard

When you buy or sell through the App, a record is published to a public real-time database (Google Firebase) so the in-app "live feed" and "leaderboard" can work. Each record may contain:

  • a shortened/masked wallet address, or a display name if you choose to set one;
  • the coin symbol(s) and mint address(es) of the trade;
  • the SOL amount and a timestamp;
  • for sells, the realized profit/loss in SOL;
  • for buys, the chosen "intensity" tag.

This information is visible to other users of the App and is pseudonymous. Do not set a display name that identifies you if you wish to remain anonymous. You can leave the display name empty to show only a masked address.

Data stored only on your device

Your portfolio (the trades you made), your chosen language, your display name, and app preferences are stored locally on your device (device storage) and are not sent to us. Uninstalling the App removes this local data.

Third-party services

The App sends requests to independent services to function. Your wallet address and/or transaction data may be processed by:

  • Solana RPC providers (e.g. Helius and a public fallback node) — to read balances and broadcast transactions;
  • Jupiter — for token data, quotes, and swap routing;
  • GeckoTerminal — for token prices;
  • RugCheck — for token safety scores;
  • Google Firebase (Realtime Database) — to host the public live feed and leaderboard.

These providers process data under their own privacy policies. The Solana blockchain itself is public and immutable.

Blockchain data is permanent

Transactions you sign are recorded on the public Solana blockchain and cannot be deleted or altered by us or anyone. Entries in the live feed/leaderboard are pseudonymous; we can remove a feed entry on reasonable request where technically possible, but on-chain records remain.

We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade any data. We do not build advertising profiles.

Children

The App is for users 18+ and is not directed to children.

Changes

We may update this Policy. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
